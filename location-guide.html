How to Find Your Latitude and Longitude

Follow these simple steps to get your location coordinates using Google Maps.

Step 1: Open Google Maps

Go to Google Maps on your browser or app.

Step 2: Find Your Location

Search for your address or click the location pin to mark your spot.

Step 3: Get Coordinates

Right-click (or long-press on mobile) the location and select "What's here?" to view the latitude and longitude.
